We are a group of highly qualified professionals who have made distinct contribution to Indian Defense, Judiciary, Corporate, Civil Services & Research Our objective is to guide and support the under privileged members of the community so that they can attend a level of self respect and dignity.

We Identify, guide, sponsor & train students aspiring to make their career in civil services, Judiciary, Indian Defence & Medical & Engineering. We have taken up this challenge from 2014, and over a period of time we have sponsored around 60 students costing us around Rs. 60 lakhs. To motivate and identify the aspirants we visit more than 20 City / Towns of Maharashtra, every year. Status of the program conducted by us:

Civil Services One - three year residential program Free Coaching & Accommodation provided. Food will be charged Process of Selection – Entrance Exam in February 1st week
Judiciary Two – Three months Coaching Free Coaching. Accommodation at Cost & availability Straight admission
JEE / IIT One year coaching Cost of coaching is subsidized Entrance exam
Summer Camp One month residential coaching Free Coaching & Accommodation provided. Food will be charged Entrance exam

Our process of sponsorship is based on the percentage of marks obtained in our “Entrance Test” and the financial capability of the student. In the year 2019-20, we intents to support around 40 students which is estimated to cost us Rs 50 Lacks /- (cost of coaching, accommodation & Food) per candidate..

Waqf Exam Free Legal & Technical Advise

Waqf is an import resources for the development of the community. We have been taking keen interest in educating and training the members of the community to develop and protect the Waqf Properties. We have a team of retired Judges, technical professional to guide the members of the community on various issues. People intending to avail free guidance and support can contact our office as mentioned on the website.

  • Training the people on Waqf law, role & responsibilities & how to develop Waqf Properties
  • Provide Technical & Legal advise and documentation on Waqf properties.
  • Guidance on developing the waqf properties.
  • Liaising with the government machinery on different issues.
