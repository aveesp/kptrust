WAQF Program

THREE YEAR SUPPORT PLAN – FOR INTERESTED STUDENT


It is Provided to:

Capable & deserving student who need more coaching and environment.

We can accommodate the student maximum of 3 years, subject to meeting the conditions.

First Year
  • Free Coaching & Accommodation, Only food shall be charged to the candidate.
  • Criteria for second year admission shall be based on Minimum percentage of marks obtained in test.
Second Year – Revision Program
  • Free Coaching & Accommodation, Only food shall be charged to the candidate.
  • Criteria for Third year admission shall be based on Minimum percentage of marks obtained in test.
Third Year – Study Circle
  • Free Coaching, Accommodation, food will be charged.
  • Training shall be based on “Study Circle” pattern. Shall consist of Test and addressing problems.
Training Approach
  • Understanding Syllabus
  • Colour coding the Topic
  • Assist in Planning & Execution
  • We ensure syllabus is completed
  • Group study
  • Group discussions
  • Presentations
  • Provide Training in Making notes Pre & Mains
  • Creating system to retrieve
  • Drafting skills
  • Writing Summary notes
  • Preparation for Personal Interview (Mock Interview)
